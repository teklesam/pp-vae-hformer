\caption[HybridBlock (HB) internal structure]{%
  \textbf{HybridBlock (HB) -- sequential convolutional and windowed self-attention
  processing.}
  The input feature map $\mathbf{f}_{\mathrm{in}}$ (illustrated by a white-ruled lung
  thumbnail) first enters the \textbf{ResBlock}: two $3{\times}3$
  convolution--GroupNorm--GELU sequences with a residual shortcut produce
  $\mathbf{f}_{\mathrm{res}}$.
  This is then processed by the \textbf{WindowAttnBlock}: after LayerNorm, the
  feature map is partitioned into non-overlapping $8{\times}8$ windows
  ($H{\times}W{\times}C \to N{\times}64{\times}C$; right panel), within which
  four-head multi-head self-attention is applied, followed by a second LayerNorm
  and a two-layer FFN (channel expansion $4{\times}$, GELU).
  An outer residual adds $\mathbf{f}_{\mathrm{res}}$ before windows are folded back
  to $H{\times}W{\times}C$, yielding $\mathbf{f}_{\mathrm{out}}$.
  Two HybridBlocks are stacked at each encoder, decoder, and bottleneck stage.}
\label{fig:arch:hybridblock}
